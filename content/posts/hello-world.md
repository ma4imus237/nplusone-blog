This is the first post on **n+1**. A space for ideas, one increment at a time.

## Why n+1?

Every interesting problem starts with a question: *what comes next?* The name is borrowed from mathematics, the inductive step, the next iteration, the perpetual move forward.

## What to expect

Short essays on technology, software engineering, and the occasional tangent into whatever catches my attention. No fixed schedule, no fixed format. Just writing.
